Italian foods and thier details:

italian cuisine Italian cuisine is a rich and diverse culinary tradition that has gained immense popularity worldwide. It is characterized by its simplicity, with many dishes having only four to eight ingredients, emphasizing the quality of the ingredients themselves rather than elaborate preparation . This philosophy is rooted in Italy's strong regional traditions, with each region boasting unique specialties and flavors
